Services

Weight Loss

Weight loss programs will help you shed your extra weight in a healthy and sustainable way. Individually designed diet plans with constant support and monitoring to help you with your weight loss journey.

Diet for Diseases

Diet for a number of health conditions such as Hypothyroidism, PCOS, Hypertension, Anemia, Lipid abnormalities, etc. Simple and easy meal plans to make compliance easy.

Diabetes Management:

These programs will help diabetic clients with limited options to enjoy tasty food and manage their blood sugar levels. Simple and easy meals and basic exercises to make living with diabetes a bit easy!

Diet for Lifestages

Diet for life stages such as pregnancy, lactation, kids nutrition, adolescence and elderly are provided based on individual requirements to suffice the nutrient need of the particular lifestage.

Weight Gain

No need to worry about your thin body. With our weight gain plans, you will start gaining weight in a healthy way keeping diseases at bay. Weight gain plans specially tailored with healthy food options to increase your muscle mass and Body weight.

Sports Nutrition

Athletic body requires proper planning of diet to meet the increased demand of nutrients as per the sport you play. Special guidance and nutrition plans for individual sport categories to boost your optimum performance, health and chances of winning.
